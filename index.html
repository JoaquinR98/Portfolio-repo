Who am I?

My name is Joaquin Rodini and I am a UX and UI Designer living in Buenos Aires who is passionate about the art of creating clean, original and great designs.


After high school and having lived abroad for a year, I began my journey by embarking on a Web Development career at a education center downtown. Soon after, I discovered about UX and was instantly drawn to it. Upon becoming more involved within the realm of UX, I found a strong passion for the design and creativity based aspects, as well as business skills such as research, communication and analysis - all of which I am currently professionally pursuing.

Let's work together!
