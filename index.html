Who am I?

My name is Joaquin Rodini and I am a UX and UI Product Designer living in Buenos Aires who is passionate about the art of creating clean, original and great designs that will delight people.


After graduating high school, I began my journey by embarking on a Web Development career at a education center downtown. Soon after achieving a strong front-end foundation, I discovered UX and was instantly drawn to it. Upon becoming more involved within the realm of UX and its User-Centered design cycle, I found a strong passion for the design and creativity based aspects, as well as its business skills such as research, communication and analysis - all of which I am currently professionally pursuing.
