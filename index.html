MyPriest

An app for catholics, made to facilitate contact with priests and discover churches.

The challenge

Since catholics today donâ€™t have a confortable and efficient way of reaching out to priests, other than going from church to church, MyPriest was made to provide a new and modern manner to do this.

Objectives

  • Design a mobile app for MyPriest that is user-friendly, efficient and aesthetically pleasing
  • App must be desirable and intuitive for all age groups

Scope

July 2021

Tools

Google forms, Miro, Figma

Team

Self-directed in collaboration with a priest

01. Research


Research goals

  • Identify the target audience for MyPriest
  • Understand catholics, habits and motivations
  • Identify frustrations and setbacks when seeking a priest for confession or councel
  • Identify which features would users find valuable for the app

Surveys

To gather both quantitative and qualitative data on potential users, I created and conducted a survey on Google forms.

I collected 141 responses from catholics online.



The key findings of the survey were:

  • 78.4% of participants who confess on a regular basis do not go to the same priest for it.
  • 30.4% of participants had or have difficulty in finding a priest to confess to.
  • 71.4% of participants would like an easier channel to contact a priest, with only 19.3% undecided.

User interviews

In order to delve into some of the results the surveys gave me and gain some insights about them, I recruited 3 participants for user interviews. Here, I focused on asking open.ended questions about their experiences to learn as much as possible about the users and validate my understanding of them.

02. Define


User personas

Based on the feedback and insights gained from the research phase, I created two personas who had the profile of someone who would use the MyPriest application. These personas demonstrate the key user goals and frustrations in my findings.

Journey map

To begin visualizing the process that a user will go through to accomplish their goal I developed a journey map. I chose Darien as the main actor of it.
