Personal project

Walla

Redesigning an exclusive accesories e-commerce platform.

Challenge

Walla is an e-commerce platform from Argentina. The brand focuses on selling high quality accesories.
The challenge was to improve the conversion rate and upgrading the visual look while maintaining the slick, elegant and exclusive identity the brand has.

Solution

The IA, navigation, user interfacte and overall UX were redesigned and improved based on insights from user research and delivered in the form of a high-fidelity, interactive prototype
