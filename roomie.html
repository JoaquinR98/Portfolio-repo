Personal project

Roomie

A shared calendar application made to help roommates and house members organize and optimize their cleaning schedules in the household.

Challenge

One day as I was dwelling online, I stumbled upon a real need of someone in need of a tool that would help them with the cleaning management of the household with her new roommate.
Given the lack of non-individual scheduling application, she was in need of one that would actually mold to her need.

Solution

A shared multi-platform scheduling application that will allow its users to set and define a clear systematic cleaning routine.
